About Me

I am a passionate first-year student with a deep enthusiasm for technology and a strong desire to make a positive impact on the community through innovative solutions.

Throughout my academic journey, I have actively pursued opportunities to engage with and contribute to the tech field. As a coordinator of the Growth Marketing Division in the Computer Science Society, I have harnessed my tech enthusiasm to create compelling online content and promote engagement.

My tech-driven mindset also led me to participate in transformative projects such as the Penang Hawker assistance and acquisition program with GrabFood Penang, where I leveraged technology to support local culture while adapting to changing circumstances.

With a continuous thirst for learning and a commitment to driving positive change, I look forward to further cultivating my tech skills and utilizing them to create meaningful solutions that benefit both the digital world and the broader community.
